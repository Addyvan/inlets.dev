A better way to connect

Secure site-to-site tunnels without the hassle of a VPN

Connect anything, anywhere.

Any two machines which have at one way network connectivity (such as your laptop and a Cloud VM with a public IP) can be used to establish a tunnel. Traffic sent to the exit node gets sent through a secure tunnel to the destination. You can use Inlets PRO to connect anything, from two private networks all the way to connecting your edge IOT devices to your cloud networks.

  • Expand your IP range, within minutes

    Use a cloud VM with a public IP to forward network traffic to your datacenter without buying new IPs

  • Save on your cloud costs by re-using your datacenter

    Don't leave your on-prem compute behind when you migrate to the cloud - inlets tunnels can connect your on-prem compute to the cloud without the hassle of managing VPNs

  • Native Kubernetes support, even behind firewalls and NAT

    Inlets PRO is a cloud native tunnel. It has first class support for Kubernetes networking. Use Inlets PRO to connect private kubernetes clusters to the internet securely.

Frequently asked questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes, you can request a free 14-day trial at https://github.com/inlets/inlets-pro

How does the licensing work?

You purchase a license code which will be delivered as a JWT token, when it expires the software will stop working, so you should renew your license and update your key when that time approaches. The license is part of the client, so you do not have to re-create your servers.

What is a tunnel-pair?

A tunnel-pair is an inlets PRO client and server. You need both to create a functioning tunnel.

What if we need custom development, support or a proof of concept?

This is available at a further cost

Do I have to expose my applications on the Internet for this to work?

No, inlets PRO can bind to a private or local ethernet adapter, so that only the control-plane is exposed, with authentication and TLS enabled.

Can I pay for inlets PRO monthly?

Inlets PRO can be purchased for 6 or 12 months for personal use, for business use, it is purchased annually.

What if I want to add more tunnel pairs during a period?

You can pay pro-rata to add more tunnels to your package.

Where can I use inlets PRO?

Inlets PRO can be used behind HTTP proxies, NAT, captive firewalls, hotel WiFi, Carrier-grade NAT, from private VPCs and from private VM networks. If you want to test your network conditions, just take out a free 14-day trial at any time.
